Here are the instructions to test the Vite logo:

1.  **Open the Website:**
    *   Open your web browser and navigate to `http://localhost:5173/`.

2.  **Verify Vite Logo Presence:**
    *   Visually confirm that the "Vite logo" image is displayed on the page. It should be one of the first two logos at the top.

3.  **Verify Vite Logo Link:**
    *   Hover your mouse cursor over the Vite logo. Confirm that it appears as a clickable link (e.g., the cursor changes to a pointer).

4.  **Test Vite Logo Navigation:**
    *   Click on the Vite logo.
    *   Observe that a new browser tab or window opens.
    *   Verify that the URL in the newly opened tab/window is `https://vite.dev`.
